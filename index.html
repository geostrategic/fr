GeoStrategic is an indie game in pixel art style. Manage your country and try to reach your goal:

  • Be the last survivor
  • Conquer the biggest surface
  • Reach a single goal (protect a country, destroy a country, defend a territory, conquer a territory)

You manage your people, you resources, your vehicles.

You manage your diplomatic relations (alliances, ennemies, embargo...)

You can:

  • Craft
  • Fish
  • Cut wood
  • Attack with your vehicles
  • Launch missiles with sub-marines

The game is made of 2 parts:

  • Step 1 : Bids
  • Step 2 : The main game
