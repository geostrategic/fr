Step 1 : BIDS

In the first part of the game, you make bids for the territories you want to acquire.

To achieve this, you have:

  • 1: a table of all the players/countries
  • 2: a table of all territories available with statistics
  • 3: the map
  • 4: your credits
  • 5: the goal to achieve

Click on the territories on the map or click on the button of the lines of a territory in table #2 to make bids for a country.

You can't own more than 3 countries.

Once you are ready, click on the button "Ask end of bids" to finish the bids.
