Overview Step 1 - Bids

Step 1 : BIDS

In the first part of the game, you make bids for the territories you want to acquire.

To achieve this, you have:

  • 1: a table of all the players/countries
  • 2: a table of all territories available with statistics
  • 3: the map
  • 4: your credits
  • 5: the goal to achieve

Click on the territories on the map or click on the button of the lines of a territory in table #2 to make bids for a country.

You can't own more than 3 countries.

Once you are ready, click on the button "Ask end of bids" to finish the bids.


Step 1: Bids

Step 2 : MAIN GAME

After bids, you enter the second part of the game: the main part of the game.

The background shows you the map with ground, cities, people, vehicles, factories...
You can :
- zoom and dezoom with keyboard ('+', '-')
- move the camera with [arrows].

You can notice 5 zones:

  • 1: information about your general resources, weekly needs and time
  • 2: group of buttons that enable you to see statistics, manage diplomatic relations, trading, see and send messages, shows information on map
  • 3: your goal to achieve
  • 4: group of buttons to select the sub-group of buttons (5)
  • 5: group of buttons to build things or give orders to your people or your vehicles

Step 2: Main game

You can increase the primary resources (wood, fishes, oil, gold) by crafting mines or with international trading.
The number of missiles can be increased by building them or by buying them with international trading.


Weekly needs

Under general resources, the weekly needs show you what your country will require to survive.

  • If you don't have enough fishes or wood at the end of the week, some of your people will die.
  • If you don't have enough oil, your vehicles won't be able to move.

Weekly needs

In this example, you can see that, every week, your 3 people will require 0 wood and 3 fishes. And you need 0 oil.

The formulas for needs are the following :

  • Fish needs: each people will need one fish every week
  • Wood needs: the capital city requires 2 woods and each city will require 1 wood very week
  • Oil needs: each tank requires 1 oil * each helicopter requires 2 oils * reach sub-marines requires 4 oils every week
